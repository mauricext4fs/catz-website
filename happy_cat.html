Origin / History

The Happy Cat is not a single meme otherwise a group of memes. It generally represent any Cat meme showing up a very happy Cat with a short statement that should bring smile to your face.

As declared on Know your meme it is said that the first public appearance of the Happy Cat meme is conflicting. The original version of the happy Cat by "FancyCat", it is said, was copied from a Russian cat food company. But it is widely known to have become famous on Something Awful Forum in 2003.

Usage

The usage seen up in this page is a unusual setting for the happy cat. The happy cat is usually used in the setting where "Friday" is announced or in case of gift, silly or ironic statement regarding ones odd fad.
