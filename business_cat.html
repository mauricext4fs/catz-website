Origin / History

On Meme Wikia it is said that the first business cat to hit the web was made by the user Apple Jax on the Something Awful Forum on first January 2011. Yes you heard that... already six years ago.

The business cat has two renewed iteration. The "meme" shown at the top uses the version derived directly from Apple Jax. Whereas this one below is from "Tom Fonder", the second most popular and recognized version of the Business Cat, which is not related directly to Apple Jax.

Usage

While the first iteration of the Business Cat is mainly used in as one Meme image containing one simple statement, the second one by Tom Fonder is mainly used in a cartoonish Meme Style which comprise many frame with a short story.

Both version of the Business Cat meme are usually variations of a cat business owner or "boss" escaping his responsibility by doing "Cat" things instead.
