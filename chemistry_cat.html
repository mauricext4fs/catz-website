Origin / History

The origin of the Chemistry Cat are not well known. However, Know your meme attribute the origin to the "Chemistry Cat" thread on Reddit on april 19th 2011.

Usage

Of all the cat memes of the Internet, the chemistry cat is certainly the one most related to statement that requires the reader to have at least attend one class of college chemistry. All statement on a chemistry cat are pun related to chemistry and more specifically the Periodic Table.
